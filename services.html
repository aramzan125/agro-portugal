Our Services

Comprehensive agricultural solutions tailored to Portugal's climate, land, and market demands.

🌾

Crop Production & Management

Full-cycle management of cereals (wheat, barley, corn), sunflowers, and legumes. We use GPS-guided planting, soil analysis, and crop rotation to maximize yield while preserving soil health across the Alentejo and Ribatejo regions.

🫒

Olive Cultivation & Olive Oil

Traditional and intensive olive grove management. From pruning and harvesting to cold-press extraction — we produce premium extra virgin olive oil certified for export to European and international markets.

🍇

Vineyard & Wine Grapes

Vineyard establishment, canopy management, and grape harvesting for Portugal's renowned wine regions. We partner with local wineries and export cooperatives.

🐄

Livestock & Dairy Operations

Cattle and sheep farming with modern dairy facilities. Animal health monitoring, pasture rotation, and EU-standard welfare practices for meat and dairy production.

💧

Irrigation & Water Management

Design and installation of drip, sprinkler, and pivot irrigation systems. Water rights consulting, reservoir management, and drought-resilience planning for Portugal's dry summers.

🌱

Organic & Sustainable Farming

Certified organic conversion, composting programs, cover cropping, and reduced chemical inputs. We help farms meet EU Green Deal sustainability targets.

🚜

Equipment & Machinery Services

Tractor, harvester, and sprayer fleet leasing. Preventive maintenance, operator training, and 24/7 field support during peak harvest seasons.

📦

Post-Harvest Processing

Cleaning, grading, packaging, and cold storage for grains, fruits, and vegetables. Quality control and traceability from farm to distribution center.

🌍

Export & Supply Chain

Logistics coordination from Portuguese fields to European ports and global markets. Customs documentation, phytosanitary certification, and buyer relationships.

📋

Land Management & Consulting

Agronomic consulting, land acquisition support, subsidy application assistance (PAC/EU funds), and long-term farm planning for landowners and investors.

👷

Seasonal Labor Coordination

Recruitment, training, and deployment of seasonal workers for harvest campaigns. We manage teams of 50–200 workers across multiple sites during peak season.

🔬

Soil & Crop Analysis

Laboratory partnerships for soil nutrient testing, pest monitoring, and yield forecasting. Data-driven recommendations for fertilization and treatment.

Need a Custom Solution?

Every farm is different. Contact us to discuss your land, crops, and goals — we'll build a plan that works.
