Who We Are

Rooted in Portuguese Soil, Growing Globally

VerdeCampo Portugal is a full-service agricultural company operating across southern and central Portugal. We combine traditional farming wisdom with modern technology to produce olives, wine grapes, cereals, and livestock — while creating meaningful employment for local and international workers.

  • EU-compliant wages and working conditions
  • Support for work permits and relocation
  • Certified organic and sustainable practices
  • Training and career development programs
